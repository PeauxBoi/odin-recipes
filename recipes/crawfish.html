    • A traditional crawfish boil is a one-pot meal that requires live crawfish, heavy dry and liquid seasonings, aromatics, and hearty sides. While recipes can be customized to taste, the core ingredient list generally includes:

    Ingredients

    • Live crawfish
    • Dry seasonings
    • Liquid seasonings
    • Aromatics
    • Hearty sides

    Steps

    1. Fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil.
    2. Add the dry seasonings and liquid seasonings to the boiling water.
    3. Add the aromatics and hearty sides to the pot.
    4. Add the live crawfish to the pot and cook according to package instructions.
    5. Drain the crawfish and serve hot.
